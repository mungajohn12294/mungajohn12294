About Online Voting

Online voting is a modern approach to elections, allowing voters to cast their ballots securely over the internet. It eliminates the need for physical polling stations, reduces costs, and enhances accessibility.

Why Online Voting Matters

  • Convenience: Vote from anywhere, anytime.
  • Security: Advanced encryption and authentication methods ensure safety.
  • Transparency: Real-time tracking and audit logs for accountability.
  • Inclusion: Provides easier access for people with disabilities and those in remote areas.
  • Faster Results: Automated counting speeds up election processes.
